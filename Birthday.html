Papa's 70th Birthday

A journey of 70 beautiful years...

↓

Dear Papa,

Happy 70th birthday. 🎂🎁❤️

It actually feels strange writing that number — 70. In my mind you’re still the same Papa I grew up with… just with your super vision that you have now after the cataract surgery, which you are extremely proud of. 😆

Papa's "Super Vision"
"Someone steady. Someone dependable. Someone who quietly worked hard so the family could move forward."

When I think about growing up, one thing that always stands out about you is how quietly you did everything for the family. You were never the loud type. If anything, most of the time the complaints probably went to Mama first... but somehow, things were always taken care of.

And then of course… the onions! 🧅🧅😱🫢
To you, onions are basically one of the greatest foods ever created — antioxidants, nutrients, health benefits, tasty, miracles… I don’t think I’ve met anyone else who defended onions as passionately as you. But yea... still not into it. 🤪

The Legendary Onions 🧅

Some of my favourite memories are the really random ones. Like your famous tug-of war with Lubby when he tried to steal the durian. I still remember watching the two of you pulling on opposite sides of the durian. It was chaotic, ridiculous, and absolutely unforgettable. 🤭

The Durian Championship

Another thing I admire about you is your passion for music 🎼🎹 When you were serving as a keyboardist in church every weekend, you were super committed. I could always see how much joy it brought you.

Keyboardist at Church 🎹

Then there’s your “signature dish” — Spam with onions and dark sauce. somehow this combination has become a family classic. 😋

And the swings you built for us at the 245 house. Those small things probably seemed simple to you, but to us it meant a lot. We spent lot of happy hours on those swings.

Your focus can be… quite legendary. Like chasing a cat in the warehouse and breaking your finger, or the period of time researching fake dead bird props to scare birds away. When you decide something needs to be done, you really go all in.

Warehouse Adventures 🐈‍⬛

Sometimes fathers don’t say a lot. But their love shows up in the things they do. Looking back now, I realise that your love has been there all along — in all the quiet ways you took care of us.

Happy 70th birthday, Papa. ❤️ I’m really grateful that you’re my father.

With much love,

Xiao Yen
